Infrastructure teams shouldn't need six dashboards

SmartCity Nexus started from a simple observation: cities already collect enormous amounts of data — it's just scattered across systems that don't talk to each other.

Our mission

One layer, not six logins

Traffic systems, energy monitors, water sensors, and safety cameras are usually built by different vendors, on different timelines, with no shared data model. City teams end up stitching together spreadsheets just to answer basic questions.

Nexus exists to remove that gap — a single, real-time layer that any existing system can plug into, and any team can actually use without a six-week training program.

What we optimize for

Three things we don't compromise on

Reliability

99.98% uptime isn't a marketing number — it's a requirement when the data behind it is traffic signals and safety alerts.

Transparency

Every alert and automation is traceable back to the raw reading that triggered it — no black-box scoring.

Openness

Standard protocols in, an open API out. Nothing about Nexus locks a city into a single vendor forever.
